# Standard Blog Footer Disclaimer

Verbatim disclaimer block that appears at the end of every TCA blog post. The compliance check (`tca-compliance-check`) Rule 7 requires this block on all blog content. The text is approved as-is — do not modify per-post.

---

## Where this gets applied

- **Blog posts:** required, verbatim, immediately after the closing essay paragraph and before the author bio block. Format with a horizontal rule above and below.
- **Lead magnet PDFs:** required, on the final content page or as the back-cover footer.
- **Email newsletter:** required at the bottom of every issue (above unsubscribe).
- **Standalone landing pages:** required in the page footer.
- **Social media posts:** **not** required inline. Profile-level disclosure (in the bio/About section) is sufficient for routine educational social posts. Inline disclosure is required only when the post triggers it: testimonials, performance claims, third-party ratings, or product comparisons.

---

## Disclaimer block — copy verbatim

```
---

*This article is published by Confluence Media Group LLC, an independent publisher of educational financial content. Thomas Clark is a Series 65 Investment Advisor Representative. The information provided is for educational and informational purposes only and is not personalized financial, tax, or legal advice. Past performance does not guarantee future results. All investing involves risk, including potential loss of principal. Consult a qualified professional before making financial decisions.*

*Confluence Media Group LLC is a separate entity from Confluence Capital Management, the investment advisory practice through which Thomas Clark provides advisory services. Advisory services are not offered through this publishing platform.*

---
```

---

## Notes for the routine

- Insert exactly as shown, including the markdown horizontal rules (`---`) above and below.
- The italic asterisks are intentional and must be preserved — the post styling renders them as italic small text.
- Do not add the date, post title, or any post-specific text inside the block. The block is identical across every post.
- The author bio block follows after this disclaimer block, separated by a second horizontal rule. The author bio comes from `/assets/author-bio.md`.
